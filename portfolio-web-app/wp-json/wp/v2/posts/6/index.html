Why we chant him<\/p>\n\n\n\n

When we know he never please.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Why we await him<\/p>\n\n\n\n

When we know he never come.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Why we call him<\/p>\n\n\n\n

When we know he never listen.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Why we have faith in him <\/p>\n\n\n\n

When he gives life to us.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

When he takes care of us.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

When he provides food to us.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

When he runs our breath.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

When he joins always to us.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Why we remember him<\/p>\n\n\n\n

When we know he is always with us.<\/p>\n\n\n\n
